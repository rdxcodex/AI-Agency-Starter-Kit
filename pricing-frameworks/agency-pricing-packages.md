# Agency Pricing Frameworks

**Purpose:** Stop pricing by the hour. Hourly pricing punishes you for being fast and efficient. Use this value-based, 3-tier framework to anchor your prices high and make your core offering look like a steal.

---

## The "Good, Better, Best" Framework

When presenting a proposal, always offer three options. This shifts the client's psychology from "Should I hire them or not?" to "Which package should I choose?"

### Tier 1: The Starter Package (The Anchor)
* **Goal:** A low-barrier entry point for skeptical clients, or a decoy to make Tier 2 look better.
* **Pricing:** $1,500 - $2,500 Setup + $199/mo
* **What's Included:**
  * Basic Web Chatbot (pre-programmed flows, no AI).
  * Simple Zapier integration (e.g., Lead form -> Google Sheet).
  * 1 round of revisions.
* **Positioning:** "This is for businesses that just need to plug a few basic leaks, but it requires more manual oversight from your team."

### Tier 2: The Growth Package (The Core Offer)
* **Goal:** This is the package you actually want them to buy. It should have the highest profit margin.
* **Pricing:** $4,000 - $6,000 Setup + $599/mo
* **What's Included:**
  * Custom AI Chatbot trained on their specific Knowledge Base.
  * Complex CRM Routing (Make.com).
  * Automated SMS Follow-ups.
  * Priority Support.
* **Positioning:** "This is our most popular option. It completely replaces the need to hire a front-desk admin for lead qualification."

### Tier 3: The Premium/Enterprise Package (The Price Anchor)
* **Goal:** To make Tier 2 look incredibly affordable. Occasionally, a whale client will actually buy this.
* **Pricing:** $10,000+ Setup + $1,500/mo
* **What's Included:**
  * Omnichannel AI Voice Agent + Web Chatbot + WhatsApp Bot.
  * Full CRM overhaul and data migration.
  * Custom Dashboard builds.
  * 24/7 dedicated Slack support channel.
* **Positioning:** "This is our 'done-for-you, white-glove' service. We essentially become your fractional Chief Automation Officer."

## Retainer Pricing Strategy

Never sell a one-off build. If you build a system for $5,000 and walk away, your MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) is zero.

**How to justify a monthly retainer ($300 - $1,500/mo):**
1. **API Costs:** You are paying for the Twilio/Make/OpenAI API usage. (Bundle this so they don't have to deal with multiple credit card charges).
2. **Hosting & Uptime:** You are actively monitoring the webhook connections to ensure the bot doesn't go down.
3. **Monthly Optimization:** You will review the chatbot/voice agent transcripts once a month, identify questions the bot failed to answer, and retrain the system.
4. **Minor Edits:** Up to 2 hours of tweaks to the workflows per month.
