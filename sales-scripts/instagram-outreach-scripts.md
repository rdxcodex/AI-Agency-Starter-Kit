# Instagram Outreach Scripts

**Purpose:** To connect with B2C businesses (Gyms, MedSpas, Restaurants, E-commerce) where the owner is likely managing the social media account directly.

---

## Strategy: The "Value First" Audit
*Do not pitch immediately in the DMs. Provide value first.*

**Step 1: The Initial Hook (Text)**
"Hey [Name/Brand], love the content you're putting out. Quick question—are you guys taking on new [Clients/Patients/Reservations] right now?"

**Step 2: The Audit Video (Send after they reply "Yes")**
"Awesome. The reason I ask is I was looking at your site and noticed a small bottleneck that's probably causing you to lose a few of those leads after hours. I actually recorded a quick 2-minute video showing how an AI chatbot could capture those leads automatically for you. Want me to send the link over?"

**Step 3: The Video Pitch (The Loom Video)**
* *Record a 2-minute Loom video of their website.*
* *Say:* "Hey guys, love your brand. I noticed you don't have a way to capture leads immediately when someone lands on this page. If I'm a customer looking to book at 11 PM, I have to fill out this long form and wait. My agency builds AI bots that sit right here [point to bottom right], answer questions instantly, and book them directly into your calendar. Let me know if you're open to chatting about how this works!"

**Step 4: The Follow-up (48 hours later)**
"Hey John, did you have a chance to watch that quick video? Let me know if you have any questions!"

---

## Strategy: The "Direct Question"
*Best for highly targeted, volume outreach.*

**Message 1:**
"Hey John, quick question about how you guys handle inbound leads?"

*(Wait for reply)*

**Message 2:**
"Thanks for getting back. I run an AI automation agency and we help [Industry, e.g., MedSpas] automate their lead follow-up. Basically, if someone DMs you or fills out a form, our AI texts them within 3 seconds to get them booked. 

Are you guys currently doing this manually, or do you have an automated system in place?"

*(Wait for reply)*

**Message 3:**
"Got it. If you're open to it, I'd love to show you a quick demo of how it works. No pressure to buy, just think it could save your front desk a ton of time. Are you free for 10 mins later this week?"
