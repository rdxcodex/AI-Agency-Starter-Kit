# LinkedIn Outreach Scripts

**Purpose:** To leverage LinkedIn's B2B environment to connect with decision-makers. LinkedIn requires a softer, more conversational approach than cold email. 

---

## Connection Request Notes (Max 300 Characters)
*Always add a note. Never pitch in the connection request.*

**Option 1 (Industry Peer):**
"Hi John, noticed we're both in the Home Services space here in Austin. Would love to connect and follow your journey with Acme Corp!"

**Option 2 (Content Engagement):**
"Hi John, loved your recent post on [Topic]. Really insightful point about [Specific detail]. Would love to add you to my network."

---

## Message 1: The Initial Hook (Send 24 hours after they accept)
*Goal: Start a conversation, do not pitch your services yet.*

**Script:**
"Thanks for connecting, John! I've been following Acme Corp for a bit. Curious, as you scale your team this year, are you guys leaning more into hiring more reps, or are you looking at automating some of your current sales processes with AI?"

## Message 2: The Soft Pitch (If they reply to Message 1)
*Goal: Transition their answer into a pain point you can solve.*

**Scenario A (They say they are hiring):**
"Makes total sense. Hiring is great for growth. A lot of the [Founders/VP Sales] I talk to find that their new reps spend 40% of their day doing manual CRM data entry instead of actually selling. We build automations that eliminate that busywork so reps can just close. Is that a bottleneck you guys are currently facing?"

**Scenario B (They say they are looking into AI):**
"Smart move. That's exactly what my agency specializes in. We've been building AI chatbots and lead-routing automations for Home Services that essentially act like a SDR that works 24/7. Have you guys mapped out exactly what processes you want to automate first, or are you still in the research phase?"

## Message 3: The Call to Action (The Ask)
*Goal: Move the conversation off LinkedIn and onto a Zoom call.*

**Script:**
"Got it. Based on what you're saying, I think I could map out a quick automation architecture that would solve that [Specific Pain Point] for you. 

Are you open to a casual 15-minute intro call next week? No hard sell, I can just show you what's working right now for other companies in your space."
