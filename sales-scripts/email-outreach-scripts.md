# Cold Email Outreach Scripts for AI Agencies

**Purpose:** To generate discovery calls by highlighting a specific pain point and offering a tangible AI solution. These templates prioritize brevity and personalization over lengthy feature lists.

---

## Template 1: The "Spotted a Leak" Approach
*Best for: Pitching AI Voice Agents or Chatbots by acting as a secret shopper.*

**Subject:** Tried calling your office / Question about Acme Corp

Hi John,

I was looking for [Service they offer, e.g., an emergency plumber] in Austin and tried calling your office yesterday after hours. It went to voicemail.

I run an AI automation agency, and we build AI voice receptionists for [Industry, e.g., home service companies]. Our agents answer the phone 24/7, sound exactly like a human, and can book appointments directly into your calendar while you sleep.

It usually saves our clients about 15-20% in lost revenue from missed calls. 

Would you be open to a 10-minute call this week to see a live demo of what this sounds like?

Best,
Alex Mercer

## Template 2: The "Process Improvement" Approach
*Best for: Pitching backend automations (Make.com/Zapier) to B2B companies.*

**Subject:** Quick question about your lead routing at Acme Corp

Hi John,

I noticed you're currently running Facebook Ads to generate leads for Acme Corp. 

Most Home Services companies we work with struggle because their leads sit in a CSV file or an inbox for hours before a sales rep reaches out. By that time, the lead has already called a competitor.

We build backend automations that take your leads, instantly qualify them using AI, and drop them into your sales team's Slack or CRM within 3 seconds of them filling out the form. 

If this could help increase your speed-to-lead, are you open to a quick chat next Tuesday?

Cheers,
Alex Mercer

## Template 3: The "Direct Value / Free Audit" Approach
*Best for: Pitching Website Redesigns or Chatbots.*

**Subject:** 3 ideas for Acme Corp's website

Hi John,

I was doing some research on Home Services companies in Austin and spent some time on your website. You have a great brand, but I noticed a couple of bottlenecks that are likely costing you conversions:

1. The site takes over 4 seconds to load on mobile (Google penalizes this).
2. There is no immediate way for a visitor to get answers to basic pricing questions without filling out a long form.

I actually recorded a quick 3-minute Loom video showing exactly how an AI Chatbot and a speed-optimization sprint could fix this and increase your inbound leads. 

Mind if I send the video over?

Best,
Alex Mercer
