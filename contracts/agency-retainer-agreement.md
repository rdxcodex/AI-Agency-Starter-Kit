## 1. Scope of Services
Agency agrees to provide AI, automation, and consulting services (the "Services") as detailed in individual Statements of Work ("SOW"). Each SOW will outline specific deliverables, timelines, and pricing, and is incorporated into this MSA by reference.

## 2. Term and Termination
* **Term:** This MSA commences on the Effective Date and shall remain in effect for an initial term of [e.g., 6 months]. Following the initial term, it will auto-renew on a month-to-month basis.
* **Termination for Convenience:** After the initial term, either party may terminate this MSA or any active SOW with [e.g., 30 days] written notice.
* **Termination for Cause:** Either party may terminate immediately upon written notice if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure the breach within 15 days.

## 3. Compensation & Fees
* **Retainer Fee:** Client agrees to pay a monthly retainer fee of $,000 for ongoing services as defined in the active SOW.
* **Software/API Costs:** Client is solely responsible for paying all third-party software licenses, API usage fees (e.g., OpenAI, Make.com, Twilio), and hosting costs required to run the automations, unless explicitly stated otherwise in the SOW.
* **Payment Terms:** Monthly retainers will be invoiced on the 1st of each month and are due Net [e.g., 15]. Late payments incur a 1.5% monthly interest charge. Agency reserves the right to suspend services and pause automations if invoices are more than 30 days past due.

## 4. Client Responsibilities
Client agrees to provide timely access to necessary systems, CRM credentials, data, and feedback. Delays caused by the Client failing to provide access or feedback within [e.g., 3 business days] will result in corresponding extensions to project timelines and do not relieve the Client of payment obligations.

## 5. Intellectual Property
* **Custom Deliverables:** Upon full payment, Agency assigns to Client the rights to use the final custom deliverables (e.g., custom prompts, specific Make.com scenarios).
* **Agency IP:** Agency retains all ownership rights to its pre-existing frameworks, methodologies, base code, and proprietary workflows used to deliver the Services. Client receives a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use these elements as integrated into the final deliverables.

## 6. Confidentiality
Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary business information, customer data, and trade secrets disclosed during the engagement. This obligation survives the termination of this Agreement.

## 7. Warranties and Limitation of Liability
* **Warranty:** Agency warrants that Services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner. 
* **AI Output Disclaimer:** Client acknowledges that Artificial Intelligence models (LLMs, Voice AI) are probabilistic and may occasionally generate inaccurate or "hallucinated" information. Agency implements strict guardrails but cannot guarantee 100% accuracy of AI outputs. Client agrees to hold Agency harmless for any damages resulting from AI-generated responses.
* **Liability Cap:** In no event shall Agency’s aggregate liability exceed the total fees paid by the Client in the [e.g., 3] months preceding the claim.

## 8. General Provisions
* **Governing Law:** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Texas, USA.
* **Entire Agreement:** This MSA and any attached SOWs constitute the entire agreement between the parties and supersede all prior discussions.
