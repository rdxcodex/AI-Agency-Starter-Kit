# Lead Qualification Calculator

This tool allows agencies to quickly score inbound leads based on objective criteria (BANT: Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline). Stop wasting hours on discovery calls with prospects who cannot afford your services or don't have the authority to sign a contract.

## 🚀 Features

* **Objective Scoring:** Assigns a mathematical score (out of 140) to remove emotion from sales qualification.
* **Actionable Advice:** Tells the sales team exactly what to do next based on the score (e.g., call immediately vs. put in email nurture).
* **Visual Status:** Uses distinct colors (Red, Orange, Blue, Gray) to instantly communicate lead temperature.
* **Modern UI:** Clean, custom select dropdowns and smooth transitions.

## 🛠️ Usage Instructions

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser.
2. Embed this on your internal agency intranet or notion page for your SDRs (Sales Development Reps) to use.
3. Simply answer the 4 multiple-choice questions after a brief email exchange or LinkedIn conversation with a prospect.
4. Follow the recommended action plan generated by the tool.

## 📝 Example Inputs & Outputs

**Example Input:**
* Company Size: $2M - $10M (30 pts)
* Budget: $5,000 - $15,000 (30 pts)
* Timeline: Within 1-3 months (20 pts)
* Authority: Manager (10 pts)

**Example Output:**
* **Total Score:** 90/140
* **Status:** ☀️ WARM LEAD
* **Action Plan:** "Action: Worth a discovery call. They likely need education on your pricing or solutions, but they are a viable prospect. Schedule a 15-min intro."
