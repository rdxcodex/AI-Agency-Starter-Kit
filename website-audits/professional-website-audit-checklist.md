# Professional Website Audit Checklist

**Purpose:** Use this comprehensive checklist to audit a prospect's existing website before pitching a redesign or automation services. This forms the basis of your "Free Audit" lead magnet or discovery call presentation.

---

## 1. Design & Aesthetics (First Impressions)
* [ ] **Modern UI:** Does the site look like it was built in the last 3 years?
* [ ] **Brand Consistency:** Are the fonts, colors, and logos consistent across all pages?
* [ ] **High-Quality Imagery:** Are they using high-res, professional photos (not obvious, cheesy stock photos)?
* [ ] **Whitespace:** Is there adequate breathing room between sections, or is it cluttered?
* [ ] **Clear Hierarchy:** Do the H1, H2, and paragraph tags guide the eye naturally down the page?

## 2. User Experience (UX) & Navigation
* [ ] **Clear "Above the Fold":** Does the hero section clearly state *what they do* and *who they do it for* without scrolling?
* [ ] **Intuitive Navigation:** Is the main menu simple (4-6 items max) and easy to understand?
* [ ] **Broken Links:** Are there any 404 errors or broken links in the header/footer?
* [ ] **Readability:** Is the font size at least 16px on desktop? Is there good contrast between text and background?
* [ ] **Contact Accessibility:** Is it incredibly easy to find a phone number, email, or contact form on every page?

## 3. Mobile Responsiveness
* [ ] **Mobile Layout:** Does the site stack correctly on a mobile screen without horizontal scrolling?
* [ ] **Touch Targets:** Are buttons and links large enough to be easily tapped with a thumb?
* [ ] **Hamburger Menu:** Does the mobile menu open and function correctly?
* [ ] **Mobile Speed:** Does the site load quickly on a 4G/5G connection?

## 4. Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Foundations
* [ ] **Title Tags:** Does the homepage have an optimized title tag (e.g., "Plumber in Dallas, TX | Company Name") rather than just "Home"?
* [ ] **Meta Descriptions:** Do pages have compelling meta descriptions that encourage click-throughs?
* [ ] **Header Structure:** Is there only ONE H1 tag per page, containing the primary keyword?
* [ ] **Image Alt Text:** Do images have descriptive alt text for accessibility and SEO?
* [ ] **SSL Certificate:** Is the site secure (HTTPS)?

## 5. Performance & Speed
* [ ] **Load Time:** Does the site visually load in under 3 seconds? (Check via Google PageSpeed Insights)
* [ ] **Image Optimization:** Are images compressed (WebP format) or are they massive 5MB files slowing down the site?
* [ ] **Bloatware:** Are there excessive unused plugins, pop-ups, or heavy animations causing lag?

## 6. Conversion Optimization & AI Readiness
* [ ] **Clear Calls to Action (CTAs):** Is there a primary, brightly colored button (e.g., "Book a Call", "Get a Quote") visible at all times?
* [ ] **Frictionless Forms:** Are contact forms short and easy to fill out (Name, Email, Phone, Message)?
* [ ] **Social Proof:** Are there testimonials, case studies, or trust badges visible near the CTAs?
* [ ] **AI Chatbot Gap:** Is there an opportunity to install an AI Chatbot to capture after-hours leads?
* [ ] **Automation Gap:** Where do form submissions go? (If they just go to a generic `info@` email, pitch CRM routing automation).
