## 1. Executive Summary
Acme Corp is currently losing an estimated ,000 per month due to delayed response times and manual customer support bottlenecks. RDX Codex Agency proposes the implementation of a custom AI Chatbot designed to provide instant, 24/7 support, qualify leads, and route complex queries to your human team seamlessly.

## 2. Project Scope
We will design, develop, and deploy a custom AI Chatbot embedded on your primary website. The chatbot will be trained exclusively on your company's knowledge base to ensure accurate, on-brand responses. 

## 3. Deliverables
* **Knowledge Base Ingestion:** Scraping and structuring up to 50 pages of your website, FAQs, and PDF documents to train the AI.
* **Custom Prompt Engineering:** Development of a robust system prompt with strict guardrails (preventing hallucination and off-topic conversations).
* **UI Customization:** Styling the chat widget to match Acme Corp's brand colors, fonts, and tone of voice.
* **Lead Capture Flow:** Pre-programmed conversation flows to collect Name, Email, and Phone Number before answering specific pricing questions.
* **CRM Integration:** Direct API connection to push captured leads into [HubSpot / Salesforce / GoHighLevel].
* **Live Handoff:** Seamless escalation protocol to transfer the chat to a human agent when the AI cannot resolve the issue.

## 4. Timeline
**Total Estimated Time: 3 Weeks**
* **Week 1:** Discovery, Knowledge Base structuring, and Initial Prompt Design.
* **Week 2:** Chatbot development, CRM integration, and internal QA testing.
* **Week 3:** Client review, widget deployment on the live site, and team training.
