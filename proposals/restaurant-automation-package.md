## 1. Executive Summary
Acme Corp is known for incredible food, but manual front-of-house operations are causing missed reservations and a lack of consistent online reviews. RDX Codex Agency proposes a specialized "Restaurant Automation Package" to digitize reservations, automate review generation, and reactivate past customers without adding any workload to your staff.

## 2. Project Scope
Implementation of a three-pillar automation system: 
1) An AI-powered reservation assistant.
2) An automated Google Review collection system.
3) A VIP customer reactivation campaign.

## 3. Deliverables
* **AI Reservation Assistant:** A web and WhatsApp-based chatbot that can answer FAQs (hours, menu, parking) and book tables directly into your reservation system (e.g., OpenTable / Resy).
* **Automated Review Collector:** A workflow that automatically sends an SMS to diners 2 hours after their reservation. Positive feedback is routed to Google/Yelp; negative feedback is routed privately to management.
* **Customer Database Setup:** Organizing your disparate customer data into a unified CRM.
* **"Slow Night" Reactivation:** A one-click SMS campaign system you can use to blast a special offer (e.g., "Free Appetizer tonight") to past customers on historically slow nights (e.g., Tuesdays).

## 4. Timeline
**Total Estimated Time: 3 Weeks**
* **Week 1:** CRM setup and integration with your existing reservation system.
* **Week 2:** Development of the AI Reservation Assistant and WhatsApp integration.
* **Week 3:** Setup of the Review Collector and Reactivation campaigns. Staff training.

## 5. Client Responsibilities
* Provide access to current reservation software and POS systems (if applicable).
* Approve the messaging copy for the Review Collector SMS.
* Ensure staff is available for a 45-minute training session on Week 3.

## 6. Success Metrics
* **Review Volume:** Increase monthly 5-star Google Reviews by [X]%.
* **Staff Efficiency:** Reduce phone calls regarding basic FAQs by 50%.
* **Table Turnover:** Generate at least 10 additional covers per month via the "Slow Night" reactivation campaigns.
