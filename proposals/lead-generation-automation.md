## 1. Executive Summary
Acme Corp's sales team is currently spending too much time manually scraping leads, sending initial outreach emails, and qualifying prospects. RDX Codex Agency proposes an end-to-end Lead Generation Automation system to scrape target prospects, personalize outreach using AI, and route only highly qualified responses directly to your sales team's calendar.

## 2. Project Scope
We will architect a fully automated outbound machine. This includes lead scraping integrations, AI-driven personalization (using OpenAI), email deliverability infrastructure, and CRM synchronization.

## 3. Deliverables
* **Data Scraping Setup:** Automated workflows to extract targeted leads from [LinkedIn Sales Navigator / Apollo.io] based on your specific Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).
* **AI Personalization Engine:** Integration with an LLM to research each lead's company and generate a highly personalized first line for the outreach email.
* **Cold Email Infrastructure:** Setup of 3 secondary domains, 9 email inboxes, and automated "warm-up" to ensure maximum deliverability and avoid spam folders.
* **Multi-Step Sequencing:** Creation of a 4-step automated email sequence that stops automatically when a prospect replies.
* **CRM Routing:** If a prospect replies positively, the system automatically tags them as a "Hot Lead" in [HubSpot / Salesforce] and notifies the assigned sales rep via Slack.

## 4. Timeline
**Total Estimated Time: 4 Weeks**
* **Week 1:** Infrastructure setup (purchasing domains, setting up inboxes, starting the warm-up process).
* **Week 2:** Defining the ICP, configuring the scraping tools, and writing the email copy.
* **Week 3:** Building the AI personalization workflows and CRM routing logic in [Make.com / Zapier].
* **Week 4:** Internal testing, client approval on the first batch of AI-generated emails, and campaign launch.

## 5. Client Responsibilities
* Provide a detailed Ideal Customer Profile (titles, industries, company size).
* Approve the base email copy frameworks by the end of Week 2.
* Handle all replies once a lead responds positively to the automated sequence.

## 6. Success Metrics
* **Deliverability:** Maintain a < 1% bounce rate and > 40% open rate.
* **Volume:** Automatically process and email [X] targeted leads per month.
* **Conversion:** Generate a minimum of [X] booked sales calls per month from the automated sequences.
