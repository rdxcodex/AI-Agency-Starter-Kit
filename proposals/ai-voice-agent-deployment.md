# Proposal: AI Voice Agent Deployment

**Prepared For:** Acme Corp / Acme Corp
**Prepared By:** RDX Codex Agency
**Date:** October 24, 2023

---

## 1. Executive Summary
Acme Corp is currently missing inbound calls during peak hours and after-hours, resulting in lost revenue and poor customer experiences. RDX Codex Agency proposes the deployment of an AI Voice Agent capable of answering calls instantly, qualifying leads, and booking appointments directly into your calendar, 24/7/365.

## 2. Project Scope
We will build a custom, conversational AI Voice Agent that sounds human, understands nuance, and follows your specific business logic. The agent will act as a frontline receptionist to handle inbound volume and route critical emergencies to your on-call staff.

## 3. Deliverables
* **Custom Voice Persona:** Selection and tuning of a highly realistic AI voice tailored to your brand identity (e.g., empathetic, professional, energetic).
* **Script & Logic Tree Development:** Mapping out the conversation flow, including objection handling, FAQs, and qualification criteria.
* **Calendar Integration:** Two-way sync with [ServiceTitan / Calendly / Acuity] to allow the agent to check availability and book live appointments.
* **Post-Call Automations:** Automatic SMS follow-ups sent to the caller and a call summary/transcript pushed to your CRM immediately after the call ends.
* **Phone Number Provisioning:** Setup of a dedicated local phone number, or configuration to forward your existing overflow calls to the AI agent.

## 4. Timeline
**Total Estimated Time: 4 Weeks**
* **Week 1:** Discovery, Voice Selection, and Scripting Workshop.
* **Week 2:** Agent development, prompt engineering, and latency optimization.
* **Week 3:** Integration with Calendar/CRM and rigorous internal "stress testing" (calling the bot with complex scenarios).
* **Week 4:** Client UAT (User Acceptance Testing), final adjustments, and live deployment.

## 5. Client Responsibilities
* Provide accurate availability hours and API access to the booking software.
* Provide a list of the top 10 most frequently asked questions and how they should be answered.
* Participate in a 1-hour "Roleplay" session during Week 3 to test the agent before it goes live.

## 6. Success Metrics
* **Missed Call Rate:** Reduce missed inbound calls to 0%.
* **Booking Rate:** AI successfully books appointments for at least [X]% of qualified inbound leads.
* **Agent Latency:** Maintain conversational latency under 800ms to ensure a natural human feel.
