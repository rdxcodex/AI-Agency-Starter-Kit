# The 4-Step Discovery Call Framework

**Purpose:** To control the frame of the sales conversation, uncover the true cost of the client's problem, and position your AI agency as the only logical solution.

---

## Step 1: The Frame Control (0-5 Mins)
*Goal: Establish authority and set the agenda so you aren't treated like a low-level order taker.*

**Script:**
"Hi John, great to meet you. Before we dive in, let me give you a quick overview of how these calls usually go. 

First, I want to ask you a few questions about your current operations—specifically how you're handling [mention their niche/problem, e.g., customer support]. I need to see under the hood of your business to understand if we can actually help you. 

If we can't, I'll point you in the direction of someone who can. If we *can* help, I'll walk you through exactly how our AI systems work, and we can decide if it makes sense to move forward. Does that sound fair?"

*Why this works: It shows you don't need their business (abundance mindset) and establishes that you are diagnosing a problem, like a doctor.*

## Step 2: The Deep Diagnosis (5-20 Mins)
*Goal: Identify the manual bottleneck and attach a dollar amount to it.*

**Key Questions:**
* "Walk me through what happens when [Trigger Event, e.g., a new lead comes in at 2 AM]?"
* "How much time is your team spending on [Manual Task] every single week?"
* "What happens when someone drops the ball on that process? How much does that cost you?"

**The Pivot:**
"So if I'm understanding correctly, your team is spending 20 hours a week on data entry, and because it's so slow, you're losing out on roughly 3-4 deals a month. At your average deal size of $5,000... this manual process is costing you about $20,000 a month in lost revenue. Is my math right there?"

*Wait for them to agree. They must say the words "Yes" to the cost of their problem.*

## Step 3: The AI Solution Pitch (20-35 Mins)
*Goal: Pitch the outcome, not the technology.*

**Script:**
"Based on what you've told me, you don't need to hire another admin assistant. You need an automated system that never sleeps. 

Here is exactly what we would build for you: We implement an AI Voice Agent that plugs directly into your phone system. When that lead calls at 2 AM, the AI answers on the first ring, sounds like a human, pre-qualifies them using your exact criteria, and books them directly into your calendar.

The result? You stop losing that $20,000 a month in missed deals, and your current team gets 20 hours back to focus on high-level closing rather than data entry."

## Step 4: The Close & Next Steps (35-45 Mins)
*Goal: Secure a commitment for the proposal review.*

**Script:**
"Does this sound like the type of system you're looking for?"

*(If Yes)*:
"Great. Building a custom system like this, integrating it into your CRM, and training the AI typically ranges between $4,000 and $6,000 depending on the exact complexity. Does that align with what you were expecting to invest to solve this $20,000/mo problem?"

*(If they agree to the price range)*:
"Perfect. My next step is to map out the exact technical architecture for your business and put together a formal proposal. I don't email proposals because they lack context. Let's get 20 minutes on the calendar for next [Day] to review it together. How does your Tuesday morning look?"

*Never end a discovery call without a scheduled follow-up on the calendar.*
