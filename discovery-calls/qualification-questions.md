# Qualification Questions (B.A.N.T. Framework for AI)

**Purpose:** To quickly determine if a prospect is worth a full 45-minute discovery call. Use these questions during a short 10-minute "triage" call or via email/LinkedIn chat.

---

## B - Budget
*Can they afford your services?*

1. **"To give you an accurate idea of what we can build, do you have a specific budget set aside for operational improvements this quarter?"**
2. **"Our custom AI solutions typically start around $3,000 for the initial build. Is that within the realm of possibility for this project?"**
3. **"How are you currently funding software or agency partnerships? Is it drawn from a specific department's budget?"**

*Red Flag:* They say they have "no budget" or expect custom software for $100/month.

## A - Authority
*Can this person actually sign the contract?*

1. **"Who else typically weighs in on decisions involving new technology or software for the company?"**
2. **"If we map out a perfect solution today, what does the internal approval process look like on your end?"**
3. **"Are you the primary driver for this initiative, or is this coming from the founders/board?"**

*Red Flag:* They say "I need to pitch this to my boss." (Action: Demand the boss be on the next call).

## N - Need
*Do they have a problem you can actually solve?*

1. **"What happens right now when a lead calls after 6 PM?"**
2. **"How many hours a week is your team spending manually copying data from Facebook to your CRM?"**
3. **"If you don't fix this inefficiency, how will it impact your growth goals for this year?"**

*Red Flag:* They just want "AI" because it's a buzzword, but they can't articulate a specific business problem.

## T - Timeline
*Is there urgency to buy NOW?*

1. **"When were you hoping to have a new automated system fully deployed and running?"**
2. **"Is there a specific event (like a busy season or a new product launch) driving the need for this right now?"**
3. **"If we provided a proposal next week, how soon would you be ready to pull the trigger?"**

*Red Flag:* They say "We are just exploring options for next year." (Action: Put them in a long-term email nurture sequence).
