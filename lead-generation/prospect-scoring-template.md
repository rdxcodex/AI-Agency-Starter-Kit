# Prospect Scoring Template (Internal)

**Purpose:** Use this spreadsheet template to score leads scraped from Apollo or LinkedIn *before* you put them in a cold email sequence.

| Company Name | Industry | Estimated Revenue | Current Tech Stack (from BuiltWith) | Score (1-10) | Outreach Angle |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Acme Plumbing** | Home Services | $2M - $5M | WordPress, No Chatbot, ServiceTitan | 9 | Voice Agent for missed calls |
| **XYZ Law Firm** | Legal | $1M - $3M | Webflow, Basic Contact Form | 8 | AI Chatbot for lead qualification |
| **Bob's Pizza** | Restaurant | < $500k | GoDaddy Website | 3 | Skip (Budget too low) |
| **Global Tech LLC** | B2B SaaS | $10M+ | HubSpot, Intercom, Salesforce | 5 | Highly saturated, requires complex pitch |

**How to Score:**
* **9-10:** Perfect ICP. High volume, clear missing tech (no chatbot), obvious budget. *Action: Highly personalized Loom video.*
* **6-8:** Good fit. *Action: Put into automated cold email sequence.*
* **1-5:** Poor fit. Too small or too complex. *Action: Delete from list.*
