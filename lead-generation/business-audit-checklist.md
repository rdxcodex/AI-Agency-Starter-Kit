# Comprehensive Business Automation Audit

**Purpose:** Use this checklist during a deep-dive discovery session with a client to identify exactly where they are leaking money and where automation can provide the highest ROI.

---

## 1. Top of Funnel (Lead Generation & Capture)
* [ ] Do leads have to wait more than 5 minutes for a response when they fill out a contact form?
* [ ] Are inbound leads tracked manually in a spreadsheet instead of a CRM?
* [ ] Does the business miss phone calls during normal operating hours?
* [ ] Is there no system in place to capture website visitors who are browsing after hours?
* [ ] Are sales reps manually copy-pasting lead data from Facebook/Google into their email platform?

## 2. Middle of Funnel (Nurturing & Booking)
* [ ] Do sales reps spend more than 1 hour a day manually writing follow-up emails?
* [ ] Is appointment scheduling done via back-and-forth emails instead of an automated booking link (Calendly/Acuity)?
* [ ] Is there a lack of automated SMS/Email reminders, resulting in a high no-show rate for meetings/appointments?
* [ ] Are contracts and proposals created manually in Word/Google Docs rather than using template software (PandaDoc/DocuSign)?

## 3. Bottom of Funnel (Onboarding & Delivery)
* [ ] Is client onboarding handled through manual emails instead of an automated intake form/portal?
* [ ] Does the team spend significant time manually generating invoices and chasing late payments?
* [ ] Are projects managed via email threads rather than a centralized, automated project management tool (Asana/ClickUp)?

## 4. Post-Sale (Retention & Reviews)
* [ ] Is there NO automated system to ask happy customers for a Google Review after a successful transaction?
* [ ] Are past customers sitting in a database without receiving automated re-engagement offers?
* [ ] Does customer support rely on humans answering repetitive, tier-1 questions (e.g., "Where is my order?", "What are your hours?")?

## Audit Conclusion & Pitch
*Count the number of checked boxes. Every checked box represents a leak in the business.*

**Script:** "Based on our audit, we've identified [X] specific areas where your team is doing manual work that software can do instantly. If we just focus on fixing [Biggest Bottleneck], we project it will save you [X] hours a week and recover [X] in lost revenue."
