# Lead Qualification Framework for Agencies

**Purpose:** Define your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) and establish clear criteria for who you will build custom automations for, and who you will politely decline.

## The "Perfect Fit" Criteria (Your ICP)
Before spending hours on a proposal, ensure the client meets this criteria:
1. **They have cash flow.** (B2B services, med-spas, established e-commerce). Do not sell automation to pre-revenue startups.
2. **They have volume.** Automation only provides ROI if there is a high volume of repetitive tasks (e.g., 50+ leads a month, 100+ support tickets). 
3. **They already use modern software.** If they run their business entirely on pen and paper and refuse to use a CRM, they are a bad fit.

## The Red Flags (Walk Away)
* **The "Idea Guy":** Wants you to build a complex SaaS product with AI on a $500 budget.
* **The Micromanager:** Wants to dictate the exact code or Make.com modules you use rather than trusting you for the outcome.
* **The "Can we do a revenue share?":** Usually means they have no money to pay you upfront.
