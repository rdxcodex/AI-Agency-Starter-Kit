# Top Website Builders for Agencies

**Purpose:** The best platforms for designing, building, and hosting client websites before layering on your AI automations.

## 1. Webflow
* **Best For:** Custom, high-end designs and animations for B2B or SaaS clients.
* **Why Agencies Use It:** Clean code export, extremely fast hosting, and total design freedom. It avoids the plugin bloat of WordPress. Excellent for SEO.
* **Drawback:** Steep learning curve for the developer.

## 2. WordPress (Self-Hosted)
* **Best For:** Content-heavy sites, blogs, and clients who demand full ownership of their tech stack.
* **Why Agencies Use It:** Powers ~40% of the internet. Infinite plugins. You can build anything on it.
* **Drawback:** Requires constant maintenance, security updates, and can become very slow if poorly optimized.

## 3. Framer
* **Best For:** Rapid deployment of beautiful, modern marketing sites.
* **Why Agencies Use It:** It feels like designing in Figma but publishes directly to the web. Unbeatable speed-to-market for agency landing pages.
* **Drawback:** CMS capabilities are not as robust as Webflow or WordPress.

## 4. Shopify
* **Best For:** E-commerce clients selling physical products.
* **Why Agencies Use It:** The absolute gold standard for e-com. Incredible app ecosystem (easy to integrate AI chatbots and SMS recovery tools).
* **Drawback:** Strict lock-in to their ecosystem and transaction fees.

## 5. GoHighLevel (Funnels)
* **Best For:** Local service businesses (Plumbers, Roofers, MedSpas).
* **Why Agencies Use It:** If you are already using GHL for their CRM, building their landing pages in the same ecosystem makes automation routing native and instant.
* **Drawback:** Design capabilities are basic compared to Webflow/Framer.
