# Top CRM Systems for AI Automation

**Purpose:** When you build lead generation or voice agents, the data has to go somewhere. These are the best CRMs to integrate your automations into.

## 1. GoHighLevel (GHL)
* **Best For:** Local businesses (Home Services, MedSpas, Gyms) and Marketing Agencies.
* **Why Agencies Use It:** It is the undisputed king of agency software. You can white-label it (put your agency's logo on it) and resell it to clients. It has built-in SMS, email, calendars, and pipelines.
* **Pricing:** $97/mo (Starter), $297/mo (Unlimited Sub-accounts).

## 2. HubSpot
* **Best For:** Mid-market to Enterprise B2B companies.
* **Why Agencies Use It:** It has the most robust API and integrates flawlessly with almost every tool on the internet. Best-in-class reporting and email tracking.
* **Pricing:** Free basic CRM; Marketing Hub starts at $800+/mo for serious usage.

## 3. Pipedrive
* **Best For:** Small, dedicated sales teams focused purely on closing deals.
* **Why Agencies Use It:** Extremely visual, easy-to-use Kanban board style pipeline. Less bloat than HubSpot, making it easier to train clients on.
* **Pricing:** Starts at $14.90/user/mo.

## 4. Salesforce
* **Best For:** Enterprise clients with massive datasets and complex hierarchies.
* **Why Agencies Use It:** If you can build AI integrations for Salesforce, you can charge premium enterprise rates. However, the learning curve for the API is steep.
* **Pricing:** Starts at $25/user/mo, but scales rapidly into the thousands.

## 5. Notion / Airtable (The "Lightweight" CRMs)
* **Best For:** Freelancers or solopreneurs who just need a database.
* **Why Agencies Use It:** Excellent for internal agency tracking (e.g., Prospect Scoring Template). Very easy to send webhook data into an Airtable base using Make.com.
* **Pricing:** Generous free tiers; Paid starts around $10-$20/mo.
