# Top Voice AI Platforms for Agencies

**Purpose:** A curated list of the best platforms to build and deploy conversational AI voice agents for inbound reception and outbound calling.

## 1. Vapi (vapi.ai)
* **Best For:** Advanced developers who need ultra-low latency and total control.
* **Why Agencies Use It:** Vapi is known for having some of the lowest latency in the industry, making the AI sound incredibly human. Great API documentation.
* **Pricing:** Pay-as-you-go per minute (varies based on the LLM and voice provider chosen).

## 2. Bland AI (bland.ai)
* **Best For:** Agencies looking for a complete suite (inbound/outbound/campaigns).
* **Why Agencies Use It:** Bland makes it very easy to launch massive outbound calling campaigns and has built-in tools for live call transfers and SMS follow-ups.
* **Pricing:** Pay-as-you-go per minute.

## 3. Retell AI (retellai.com)
* **Best For:** Building highly conversational agents with robust interruption handling.
* **Why Agencies Use It:** Excellent at handling humans interrupting the bot mid-sentence, which is crucial for realistic conversations.
* **Pricing:** Pay-as-you-go per minute.

## 4. Synthflow AI (synthflow.ai)
* **Best For:** No-code agencies and beginners.
* **Why Agencies Use It:** Very user-friendly interface. You can build a voice agent without writing a single line of code, making it perfect for rapid prototyping.
* **Pricing:** Subscription plans starting around $29/mo (includes a bucket of minutes).

## 5. ElevenLabs (Voice Provider)
* **Best For:** Sourcing the most realistic, cloned, or custom voices.
* **Why Agencies Use It:** While not a standalone agent builder (though they are expanding), ElevenLabs provides the absolute best Text-to-Speech (TTS) voices. You plug their API keys into platforms like Vapi or Make.com.
* **Pricing:** Free tier available; Creator plan at $22/mo.
