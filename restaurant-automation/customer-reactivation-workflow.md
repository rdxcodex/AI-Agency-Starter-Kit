# The "Slow Night" Customer Reactivation Campaign

**Purpose:** To turn a slow Tuesday night into a profitable evening by sending a targeted, automated SMS blast to past customers.

## 1. The Database Segmentation
* **Goal:** Do not spam your entire list. Segment users based on their behavior.
* **Filter in CRM:**
  * Tag = `Past Customer`
  * Last Visit = `> 30 days ago`
  * Has previously ordered on a Weekday (Optional)

## 2. The Campaign Execution (Manual Trigger)
* **Trigger:** The Restaurant Manager clicks "Send Slow Night Blast" in their CRM dashboard at 3:00 PM on a slow Tuesday.

## 3. The SMS Blast
* **Message Body (Example A - Free App):**
  > "Hey {{Name}}, it's [Manager Name] from [Restaurant]. We haven't seen you in a while! If you come in for dinner tonight, show this text to your server for a free [Popular Appetizer] on the house. To grab a table, tap here: [Reservation Link]"
* **Message Body (Example B - BOGO):**
  > "Hey {{Name}}, tonight is locals night at [Restaurant]! Buy any entree, get the second half off. Just reply 'YES' to this text if you're coming so I can save you a table."

## 4. The Automated Reply Handling
* **Trigger:** Customer replies "YES".
* **Action 1:** Send Confirmation SMS.
  > "Awesome, got you down. See you tonight! Let the host know you're on the VIP list."
* **Action 2:** Add to Google Sheet / CRM list.
  > Add `{{Customer_Name}}` to the "VIP List" sheet so the hostess can mark them off when they arrive.

## 5. ROI Tracking (Proving Agency Value)
* Ensure the client tracks how many people actually redeemed the offer.
* **Calculation:** (Total Redeemed * Average Order Value) - SMS Costs = Pure Profit generated by your automation. Use this for your case studies!
