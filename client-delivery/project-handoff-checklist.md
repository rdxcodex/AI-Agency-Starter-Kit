# Project Handoff Checklist

**Purpose:** To ensure a smooth transition when delivering the final automation/AI project to the client, minimizing post-launch bug reports and endless "how do I do this?" emails.

---

## 1. Pre-Launch QA (Internal)
* [ ] **Stress Test:** Have 3 different team members interact with the Voice/Chatbot trying to break it.
* [ ] **Data Flow Check:** Submit a test lead. Did it appear in the CRM correctly? Was the SMS triggered?
* [ ] **Error Handling:** Intentionally input a bad email/phone number. Did the system handle the error gracefully without crashing?

## 2. The Handoff Meeting (Live on Zoom)
* [ ] **Live Demo:** Walk the client through the final product live on the call.
* [ ] **Admin Walkthrough:** Show them how to access the backend (e.g., how to see Chatbot transcripts, where leads are stored).
* [ ] **Expectation Setting:** Remind them of the SLA (Service Level Agreement). E.g., "If you need a change made, please email support, do not text me on weekends."
* [ ] **The "Upsell" Seed:** "Now that the top-of-funnel is automated, our next step next quarter should be automating your onboarding."

## 3. Deliverables Provided to Client
* [ ] Final Training Video (Loom recording of the dashboard).
* [ ] SOP / Training Guide PDF (See `training-guide-template.md`).
* [ ] Login Credentials (if applicable, transferred securely via LastPass).
* [ ] Final Invoice sent via Stripe/Quickbooks.

## 4. Post-Launch Follow-Up
* [ ] **Day 3 Check-in:** Email to ask how the system is running and if they have any initial questions.
* [ ] **Day 14 Review:** Check the logs internally to ensure no APIs are failing.
* [ ] **Day 30 Strategy Call:** Review the metrics, ask for a testimonial/case study, and pitch the retainer or next project.
