## 🛠️ Daily Operations (For Your Staff)

### 1. How to View Chat Transcripts
If a customer claims they spoke to the bot, you can verify the conversation:
1. Log in to the Dashboard.
2. Click on the "Transcripts" or "Conversations" tab on the left menu.
3. Search by the customer's phone number or name.

### 2. How Leads are Routed
When the AI collects a qualified lead, it will automatically:
1. Create a new Contact in your CRM under the "New Leads" pipeline stage.
2. Send an automated SMS to your Sales Manager's phone: `555-0199`.
*Action Required:* Your sales team should call these leads within 15 minutes of receiving the SMS for the highest conversion rate.

### 3. How to Pause the Automations
If you are running a special event or need to temporarily stop the automated follow-up texts:
1. Log in to the CRM Dashboard.
2. Go to "Automations" -> "Workflows".
3. Toggle the switch next to "Review Collection Workflow" from ON to OFF.
4. *Remember to turn it back on the next day!*

## 🚨 Troubleshooting & Support

**"The bot answered a question incorrectly."**
* **Fix:** Do not panic. AI learns over time. Please screenshot the transcript and email it to `support@[rdxcodex].com`. We will update the Knowledge Base and push a fix within 24 hours.

**"Leads are not showing up in the CRM."**
* **Fix:** First, check if your CRM subscription is active. If it is, contact us immediately. This usually means an API key needs to be refreshed.
